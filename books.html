  • Blockade Billy

    • Stephen King

    A quick read (1-2hr) by Stephen King that I couldn't put down. If you like baseball, you can't go wrong with this one.

  • Reality Is Not What It Seems

    • Carlo Rovelli

    An introduction to quantum physics (no math background required) that I found really interesting. In my opinion, Rovelli focuses too much on the developmental history of the topic rather than the actual semantics of quantum physics. Nonetheless, was cool reading about the progression of thought from Newton and Bohr to Einstein and Feynman.

  • A Random Walk Down Wall Street

    • Burton Malkiel

    Fun introduction to many basic must-know concepts of the finance world. Utilizes a bunch of cool historical events from the street to explain abstract topics. Issued as part of many finance classes in university.
